Who we are

We’re Non-Profit Charity & NGO Organization

Shahan Welfare is a non-profit charity and NGO organization dedicated to supporting people in extreme need. Our mission is to empower underprivileged communities through sustainable initiatives, education, and healthcare programs. Join us in making the world a better place, where everyone gets the opportunity to live with dignity.

What We Do

Provide aid to communities affected by natural disasters, conflict, and poverty.

Implement educational programs for children and adults.

Promote environmental sustainability and conservation.

Foster community-driven solutions to local challenges.

Our Mission

Shahan Welfare is committed to reducing poverty, improving access to essential services, and promoting equality. Through our network of dedicated volunteers, partners, and donors, we work to uplift communities facing severe hardships.

Shahan Welfare is a non-profit organization dedicated to supporting people in extreme need. Our mission is to provide essential resources and services to underprivileged communities through programs focused on healthcare, education, and emergency relief.

You can make a donation through our secure online platform using credit/debit cards, PayPal, or bank transfer. Additionally, you can set up continuous donations to provide ongoing support.

Shahan Welfare funds a variety of projects including medical camps, educational scholarships, disaster relief efforts, and community development programs. These projects aim to address urgent needs and create long-term positive change in the communities we serve.

There are several ways to get involved with Shahan Welfare. You can volunteer your time, participate in our fundraising events, or become a recurring donor. Visit our page to learn more about opportunities to contribute and make a difference.
